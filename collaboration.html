Collaboration

Kalo is leading the way in freelancer management, working with some of the biggest global brands including Google, Airbnb and Expedia to manage their external workforce from onboarding through to payments.

At Kalo, I’ve led the design of a dramatically more scalable product as the company has seen significant growth – now with hundreds of teams using the web interface to manage their freelancers daily.

I’ve been responsible for using data in our design process to help drive decisions, adopting user research and spending time speaking to our users. I’ve also defined and evolved our design system.

Another key part of my role at Kalo has been focused on hiring many talented people to work on design, user experience, and other product roles for both our London and San Francisco offices.

Easy Task Creation

Task creation is one of the key features of the Kalo platform, and critical to our growth. Based on extensive user research and user metrics, we rethought the process of task creation in the platform to be streamlined for the end user.

Creating a Freelancer Identity

One of our most recent objectives has been to drive increased engagement with freelancers – encouraging them to contribute more information and ensure their availability is always up to date.
