Hackdays

I'm a huge advocate of company hackdays and recently started heading up the Pusher hackday committee. Our main focus is to make them inclusive and enable all areas of the business to contribute as part of a team.

Our hackdays run over two days and usually consit of teams of three. Below are a few rough prototypes that I worked on as part of the hackdays.

Hand Solo

Hand Solo was a project we worked on over two hackdays. One of the requirements of the project was to use our own tech. We came up with the concept of connecting users throughout the world through a real life robotic arm game in realtime.

Quill Email Recycling

The theme of this hackday was ‘Out of Time’ so we decided to build an app that would help you delete all of your unwanted/ old emails from your inbox. The idea is that as they’re deleted they get recycled to create plants as with letters in the real world.
